Soya Burguer


Description

If you're feeling like a hearty, get your hands dirty kind of vegan burger, you've come to the right recipe! These soya mince burgers are filling, easy to prepare and delicious. You can use any soya mince, but be sure to read my tips down below!


Ingredients

  • 400 g Soya Mince pulse into small pieces.
  • 2 Flax eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon Marmite
  • 2 Tablespoon Bread crumbs
  • 1 Tablespoon Soya Sauce
  • ¼ Teaspoon Cumin
  • ¼ Teaspoon Coriander
  • ¼ Teaspoon Salt
  • ¼ Teaspoon Pepper

Steps

  1. Using a blender, pulse your soya mince into small bits.
  2. In a bowl mix all of the ingredients together.
  3. Form 4 patties by pushing the mix firmly into a round mold.
  4. Optional step - Leave them in the fridge for 15 minutes to firm up. This can help them hold their shape.
  5. Fry up your burger patties in a pan (with oil) over medium heat for about 3 minutes each side. Drizzle your favourite sauce over the patties in the last minute!
  6. Assemble the burgers the way you like them.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
