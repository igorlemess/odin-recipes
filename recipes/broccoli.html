Description

Spicy Garlic Broccoli Dish


Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound fresh broccoli, cut into spears
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a nonstick pan
  2. Add garlic and pepper flakes to the hot oil and cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  3. Add broccoli and stir until well coated in oil
  4. Add water and cover the pan.
  5. Steam until tender but not mushy, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add salt and pepper.
